# 📉 Customer Churn Analysis | Power BI

## Overview

This project analyzes customer churn in a banking environment using Power BI. The dashboard helps identify customer segments with higher attrition risk and supports data-driven retention strategies.

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and customer lifetime value. Banks need to identify which customer segments are more likely to leave and understand the factors influencing churn.

---

## Project Objective

- Measure overall customer churn
- Analyze churn across demographics
- Compare customer behavior by geography
- Evaluate the impact of account balance and credit card ownership
- Identify high-risk customer segments

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
- Excel

---

## Key Metrics

- Overall Churn Rate
- Customer Count
- Active Customers
- Credit Card Holders
- Country-wise Distribution
- Product-wise Segmentation

---

## Key Insights

- Overall churn rate: **20.4%**
- Higher churn observed in customers aged 51–60.
- Lower and mid-range account balances showed greater churn risk.
- France, Germany, and Spain were compared for customer distribution.
- Product and credit card ownership patterns highlighted differences in customer retention.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard enables organizations to:

- Identify high-risk customer groups
- Improve retention strategies
- Support targeted marketing campaigns
- Monitor churn trends over time
- Make data-driven customer experience decisions
